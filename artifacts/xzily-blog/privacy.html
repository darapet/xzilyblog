Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Xzily ("we", "us") handles information in this preview build of the site.

1. What this preview stores

This version of Xzily is a frontend-only preview. There is currently no backend server or database. Any account details, likes, bookmarks, comments, or subscriber emails you enter are stored solely in your own browser's local storage and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

2. Clearing your data

Clearing your browser's site data, or using a different browser or device, will remove all locally stored activity with no way for us to recover it.

3. Future backend

When Xzily launches its backend service, this policy will be updated to describe what account and usage data is collected, how long it is retained, and how you can request deletion.

4. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via our contact page.
