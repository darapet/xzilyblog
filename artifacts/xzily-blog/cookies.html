Cookie Policy

Last updated July 2026

The Educative Blog's preview build does not set tracking or advertising cookies.

1. Local storage, not cookies

Instead of cookies, this preview uses your browser's localStorage to remember your session, likes, bookmarks, and comments. This data stays on your device and is not read by any server.

2. Fonts

We load the Fraunces and Inter typefaces from Google Fonts, which may set its own cookies under Google's policies.

3. Future analytics

If analytics or advertising cookies are introduced later, this policy will be updated and a consent banner will be added.
